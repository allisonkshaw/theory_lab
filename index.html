UMN Biology: Theory Under Construction

About

Welcome to Theory Group! We are an informal theory and modeling lab group joint meeting that was started by Allison Shaw, Emma Goldberg, and Yaniv Brandvain. The aim is to create a forum for providing feedback on ongoing modeling work. (This is not a seminar series with polished talks.)

We are very pleased that some work presented at theory group has eventually progressed to be a finished project. Click here to see publications that have benefited from feedback during theory group.

Participants are primarily in EEB and PMB, but all with aligned goals and interests are welcome. Email if you would like to join the mailing list.

Current organizers: Jeremy Chacon and Amanda Gorton
